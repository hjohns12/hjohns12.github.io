Far-right Youtube influencers

> View Code

To support the Media Manipulation Initiative at Data & Society, I extracted, cleaned, and visualized video metadata uploaded by a far-right influencer on Youtube, Alex Jones. Using the Youtube Data API, I first extracted IDs for all the videos uploaded to theAlexJonesChannel between January 1st, 2015 and May 4th, 2018. After cleaning the raw video data from a nested JSON format to a tidy data format, I performed a comparative analysis of videos with mainstream media tags against those without.

Made with: R/Python
