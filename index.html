I'm Hope Johnson.

I am a queer abolitionist chef, organizer, and artist living in Washington, D.C. I have particular interests in collective care-taking, fermentation, and disability justice.

Before becoming a chef, I worked as a data scientist for a number of organizations, most recently UCLA Law School's COVID-19 Behind Bars Project. In the past, I have worked on data projects related to police misconduct, digital privacy, and prison abolition.

I am open to collaborating on art projects, food projects, data projects, and other things too! The best way to reach me is via email: hopecaneel@gmail.com.
