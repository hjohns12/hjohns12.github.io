Hello, world! I'm Hope.

I work on projects that support community resilience and promote distributive justice.

I do statistics and data science at Mathematica Policy Research. At MPR I aggregate, describe, analyze, and sometimes simulate public health data. Outside of business hours, I work on projects related to police accountability, media manipulation by the alt-right, and digital privacy and equity.

Previously, I was an undergraduate at Macalester College where I received degrees in statistics and economics.

I spend most of my time in Boston, MA and Brooklyn, NY.
